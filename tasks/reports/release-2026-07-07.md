## What's in this RC (highlights)
- **Antigravity — live Model Quota (opt-in).** Real per-model-group weekly + 5-hour limits
  (Gemini, Claude&GPT) with reset countdowns, fetched from the **local** Antigravity
  `language_server` connect-rpc (`RetrieveUserQuotaSummary`, csrf-authed, 127.0.0.1-scoped
  TLS). The Google `ya29` OAuth token is never read, sent, or stored. Behind
  `@AppStorage("antigravityOnlineQuotaEnabled")` (default OFF); degrades silently when the
  Antigravity app is closed. `.providerReported` confidence.
- **Cursor — accurate plan label** via opt-in `full_stripe_profile` (same Bearer JWT); no
  fabricated gauge for uncapped accounts.
- **Codex estimated USD cost**, **Cursor local connector**, **honest connection UX**,
  **Padzy "aitracker" design compliance** (from the 2026-07-06 round).
- **Critical fix (`5cc1624`):** subprocess pipe-buffer **deadlock** in the Antigravity
  discovery that hung the entire sync (all providers UNAVAILABLE / "SYNCED NEVER") whenever
  the online toggle was on. Root-caused, fixed (drain stdout before `waitUntilExit` + watchdog
  timeout), and covered by 2 regression tests.
